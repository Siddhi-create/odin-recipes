Lasagna

Description

Prepare the Sauce: Cook the ground beef and sausage with onions and garlic. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and spices. Let it simmer. Cook the Noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles until al dente. Mix the Cheese: Combine ricotta cheese with eggs, parsley, and some Parmesan. Assemble the Lasagna: Layer the sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a baking dish. Repeat the layers and finish with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
